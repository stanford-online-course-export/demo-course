Welcome to Demo Course! This course starts today, and will run indefinitely.

Please complete the pre-course survey and then dive in! Course content can be accessed through the Courseware tab. The Courseware page is divided into sections, which you can view in the lefthand column. Clicking on a section causes it to expand and display subsections within the section. Click on a subsection to view units within the subsection. A unit will contain various components of content, such as problems, text, videos, iframed PDFs, and so on.

For those interested in more of a structured experience, click on the Syllabus at the top of the page to view a proposed outline which you can follow as you see fit.

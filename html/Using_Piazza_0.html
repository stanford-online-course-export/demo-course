If you prefer, it is possible to use alternative Discussion forum providers for your course. Keep in mind that you will not be able to use the inline discussion component on pages in your course if you use an alternative forum provider.

Piazza can be integrated into your course through an LTI component. Instructions for integrating Piazza are available here:
https://s3.amazonaws.com/piazza-materials/LTI-Setup/AddingPiazzatoedX.pdf

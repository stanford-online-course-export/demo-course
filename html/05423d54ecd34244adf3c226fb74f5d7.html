setting up a/b testing on your course site

A/B testing is a great way to see what works and what doesn't in a course.  You may want to offer slight variations on practice problems to your students and see which ones lead to better results.  Or you may want to show different video content - say a shorter versus a longer video - in order to see which ones students retain more information from.  This function allows you to set up such a test in your course and see what happens when randomly assigned participants view one version versus another.

You can hold an A/B test between two different subsections, two different units or two different components.  Click on the subsequent units and subsections to view illustrations of these kinds of A/B tests.

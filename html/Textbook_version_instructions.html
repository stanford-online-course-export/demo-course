The textbook you create will display as a tab on the course site.  Please visit https://class.stanford.edu/courses/StanfordOnline/OpenEdX/Demo/pdfbook/0/ to see the Demo Course's "Navigating Your Course" PDF textbook.

To create a textbook for your students, the best process is to have PDFs for each chapter of the textbook.  You'll need two things: a name for each chapter, and either already-uploaded PDFs, or PDFs ready to be uploaded.  

Once you have your textbook content in this format, you can click on the "Content" drop-down menu, select "Textbook" and then upload your PDFs for each chapter.  


Give the chapters names and click "Save" when you're done to create your textbook from there. 


The complete EdX instructions for adding a textbook can be found here.

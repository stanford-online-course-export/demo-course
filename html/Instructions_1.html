The Completion checkbox is not available by default, but can be added to your course by going to Settings > Advanced Settings in Studio, and adding "done" to the Advanced Module List setting.  

Save your changes when you're done, and then go to a unit in your course.  Click "Advanced" from the "Add New Component" options, and select "Completion" from the available advanced components.

This will add a "Mark as complete" component to your course, which contains a button that people can click to mark as complete, or click again to mark as something they still need to do.

This component is not customizable in any way, but may provide additional functionality which participants may find helpful.

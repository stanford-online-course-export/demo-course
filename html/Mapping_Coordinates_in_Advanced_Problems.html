Tip for Mapping coordinates in advanced problems

One bit of information that may help you when you are tailoring these problems is that some of them rely on coordinate mapping.  You can determine the coordinates of an image by starting from the top left corner of an image.  The top left corner of any image will have 0,0 as its starting coordinates, with the first 0 referring to a plotted point on the horizontal placement (or x-axis) of your image, and the second 0 referring to a plotted point on the vertical placement (or y-axis) of your image.  In this way, you can draw shapes with the coordinates, which you specify in your code, and you can use that identified space to have some action occur there. 

To learn more about coordinate mapping, feel free to visit the page at http://www.w3schools.com/TAGS/att_area_coords.asp.

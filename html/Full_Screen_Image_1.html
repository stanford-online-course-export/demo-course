Full Screen Image

Here we have a temple from Angkor Thom.  If you click to view the full screen image, you can see more clearly and in greater detail how the temple is composed of thousands of individual rocks.  The entire series of temples were composed of all of these individual pieces.   

Over time, workers in Cambodia have painstakingly pieced back together many of the temples, bit by bit.  While this is a job of epic scale, the workers' task is made a little easier by the fact that each rock is numbered, so in effect, what we have is a gigantic puzzle.

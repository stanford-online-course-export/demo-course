Please complete the pre-course survey below. Pre-course surveys are standard practice in OpenEdX courses, and you get to customize the pre-course survey to best fit your course's needs. This can be a great way to learn more about your participants and better understand the community you're instructing.

Click on the HTML component in a unit, then select "Anonymized User ID" from the HTML template options.

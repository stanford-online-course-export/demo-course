Iframing in course content is easy and creates a slick view.  To do this, first upload your file to your course.  Go to Content > Files & Uploads.  Browse the files on your computer and find the one you want.  Upload your file.  After you do that, you'll see a window displaying a short URL for your file.  Copy this short URL.  Go to a unit you have created in a subsection in your Course Outline.  Click to add an HTML component to it.  Once that opens, click "Edit" and then click "HTML".  From here, you can now enter the following iframe tag:

<iframe src="http://yourshortURL.PDF" width="800" height="600"/>

Save your changes to the component and you will now see an embedded document in the unit.  Feel free to adjust the width and height as you see fit.

For more information on adding files to your course, see http://edx.readthedocs.org/projects/ca/en/latest/creating_content/course_files.html.

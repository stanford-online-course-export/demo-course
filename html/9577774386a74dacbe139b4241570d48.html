Once you've set up the group configuration, it's easy to set up your A/B test. In a unit page, click to add an Advanced component and choose Content Experiment. You'll be alerted that you must select a Group Configuration. Choose the one you want, then click Save.

Now you can add content to your experiment. Click "View" to enter the nested component view. There will be a container for each group you configured. In our sample, we have Group 1 and Group 2. Add content as you would normally. See the screenshot below:

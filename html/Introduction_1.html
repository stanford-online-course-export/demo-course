Introduction to Tables of Contents

You can add a Table of Contents to your videos to let students navigate easily to the part they are interested in. A Table of Contents is similar to a transcript in that both contain clickable links that are tied to specific points in the video. It is possible to provide a Table of Contents in addition to a timed transcript – users switch between the two with the CC button in the video player.

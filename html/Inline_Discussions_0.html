inline discussions

These are the discussions that you can add to a specific unit in your Course Outline.  Discussion components allow you and your students to have focused conversations within a unit, and can be a good way to have discussions around a particular bit of text, image, video or question or quiz.  Discussions that are added to units also show up in the main Discussion page.

By default, the titles they take are a bit obscure. As a result, if you add a discussion component to a unit, it's a good idea to also edit the title of the discussion component's category.  To do that, head to the subsection that you added a discussion component to.  Click to edit the discussion component.

You'll see a "Category" and "Subcategory" field. 

Change these to something more meaningful to your course, and save your changes when you're done.  For example, I have a discussion component added to the Images subsection.  I've updated the category and subcategory names as you can see in this screenshot:

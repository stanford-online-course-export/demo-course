Built-in discussion feature

Every OpenEdX course comes with a built-in discussion page, which your students can use to interact with each other.  This page is also the best way for students to contact the course team.  If you provide alternate methods for them to contact you through, that's all the better, but if you have no alternatives, then this allows for a public space within which they can ask a question and get an answer from someone on your course team. 

Students should add [STAFF] to the subject heading of their discussion, so that you and your team can then easily search for [STAFF] discussions and respond to those needing your attention.
